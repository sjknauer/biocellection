About BioCellection

We are engineering a platform technology that enables large quantity biosynthesis of valuable products from algae growing on CO2 exhaust. Our plan is to create commercial-scale algae bioreactors that can utilize exhaust from emission-intensive vehicles and appliances to produce algal biomass of high value. This system aims to recycle the CO2 produced by large vehicles and appliances, such as barges, trains, heavy trucks, and municipal lawn mowers, into algal biomass, which can be harvested and converted into biofuels that can be resupplied into the vehicle. Building on top of this basic platform, microalgae used in the system can be genetically engineered to produce valuable proteins, oils, cosmetic extracts, and other biological products, which can be sold at a higher mark-up. By bringing bioreactors out of massive, isolated facilities and installing them directly onto the vehicles and appliances that can use the products directly, we can also eliminate the need for costly centralized biofuel refinement and distribution. This commercial-level bioreactor can transform many current greenhouse gas generating vehicles and appliances into hosts for algal biosynthesis.

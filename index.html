SAVING OUR OCEANS


BioCellection uses cutting edge techniques in biology to synthesize single cell protein and high value products for aquaculture while utilizing a platform technology that enables high efficiency microbial culturing on plastic waste carbon sources. We are producing economic, sustainable, and consistent fish aquafeed components to ensure fish health.
As a proof of concept, we have demonstrated that our single cell protein results in similar salmon growth rates as fishmeal, but at a 73% lower mortality rate in controlled fish trials. We are currently building a minimally viable product by optimizing our microbes.
We envision the future of our oceans to be sustained through biotech innovation.
